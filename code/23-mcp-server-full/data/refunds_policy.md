# Acme refunds policy

## Eligibility

Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase.

## Auto-approval threshold

Refunds at or below **$1 000** are auto-approved and processed immediately.
Refunds above $1 000 require manager approval and are routed to the billing
queue.

## Annual vs monthly

- Annual plans: pro-rated refunds.
- Monthly plans: all-or-nothing for the current period.

## Processing time

Auto-approved refunds appear on the customer's statement within 3–5 business
days. Manager-approved refunds add 1–2 business days for the review step.

## Office hours

Human review runs 09:00–18:00 IST, Monday through Friday.
